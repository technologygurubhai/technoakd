Our Courses

Bachelor of Science in Nursing (BSc Nursing)

Our BSc Nursing program is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of nursing principles and practices. Through a combination of classroom instruction, laboratory sessions, and clinical rotations, students develop the knowledge and skills necessary for a successful career in nursing.

Requirements:

  • HS 40-50% in Science background

General Nursing and Midwifery (GNM)

Our GNM program prepares students to deliver compassionate and competent nursing care in a variety of healthcare settings. With a focus on practical training and clinical experience, students graduate ready to meet the challenges of modern healthcare.

Requirements:

  • HS 40-50% marks in any background
