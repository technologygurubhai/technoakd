About the Gurukul

Bachelor of Pharmacy (B.Pharm) and Diploma in Pharmacy (D.Pharm) programs atGurukul Institute of Medical Science cater to aspiring pharmaceutical professionals. Our institution provides comprehensive education and training in the field of pharmacy, preparing students for rewarding careers in the pharmaceutical industry and healthcare sector.


Eligibility criteria for admission include completion of Higher Secondary education with a Science background, with a minimum aggregate of 40-50%. We strive to nurture a conducive learning environment where students can develop both their theoretical knowledge and practical skills, equipping them to excel in the dynamic pharmaceutical landscape. Join us in shaping the future of pharmacy.
