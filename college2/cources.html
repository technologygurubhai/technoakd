Advanced Courses

Explore our advanced courses designed to enhance your knowledge and skills in the field of Pharmacy.

D.Pharm (2 Years)

D Pharma course is a 2-year diploma-level course in the field of Pharmacy that trains students in various aspects of pharmaceutical science, including pharmacy practice, pharmacology, pharmaceutical chemistry, and pharmacognosy.

Qualification Needed:

HS with 40-50% marks in Science Background

B.Pharm (4 Years)

B Pharma, also known as Bachelor of Pharmacy, is a 4-year UG degree in the field of Pharmacy. B Pharmacy focuses on various aspects of pharmaceutical sciences, including pharmacology, medicinal chemistry, pharmaceutical analysis, pharmacognosy, pharmaceutics, and pharmacy practice.

Qualification Needed:

HS with 40-50% marks in Science Background
