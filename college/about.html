About Us


Welcome to Bangiyo Gurukul Ashram, where we are dedicated to shaping the future of education. Established with a vision to empower educators and transform learning experiences, we offer comprehensive programs in Bachelor of Education (B.Ed) and Diploma in Elementary Education (D.El.Ed).

At Bangiyo Gurukul Ashram , we believe in fostering an environment that nurtures intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and a passion for teaching. Our experienced faculty members are committed to providing innovative teaching methodologies and personalized support to help students thrive academically and professionally.

With state-of-the-art facilities, modern teaching techniques, and a vibrant campus community, we strive to create an enriching learning environment where students can explore their potential and become competent educators equipped to make a positive impact in the field of education. Join us at Bangiyo Gurukul Ashram and embark on a journey of knowledge, growth, and endless possibilities in the realm of education.
